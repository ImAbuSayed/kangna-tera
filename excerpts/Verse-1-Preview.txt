Kalli raat vich gaddi main chalaanda slow,
Teri yaad satave jadon neon glow,
Tere shehar di hawa vich dark romance,
Main labhda haan tainu in the trance,
Cold breeze vich udde teri kaali chunni,
Main aashiq crazy meri gall nahio sunni,
Tere piche main chadd da my duniya saari,
My crazy love di eh khed nyari.
