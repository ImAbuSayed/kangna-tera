Kangna tera ni clear ishaare kude,
Wild aashiq main ginda sitaare kude,
Teri beauty ne kitta my mind block,
Main vekhda tainu round the clock.
Sari duniya leave karke main aunda tere kol,
My passionate heart karda sweet control,
Kangna tera ni shining bright in the night,
Saeed labhda tainu make it right.
